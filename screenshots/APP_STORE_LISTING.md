## Description

Set sail with Buccaneer Wallet — the self-custodial Bitcoin wallet that makes Lightning payments fast, fun, and secure.

Whether you're sending sats to a friend, paying for your morning coffee, or stacking sats for the future, Buccaneer puts you in control of your Bitcoin with a nautical twist.

KEY FEATURES:

- Instant Payments — Send and receive Bitcoin over the Lightning Network in seconds, with real-time fee estimates before you confirm.

- QR Code Scanner — Scan any Lightning invoice, LNURL, Lightning Address, or on-chain address to pay instantly.

- Self-Custodial — Your keys, your coins. Buccaneer never holds your funds. Back up your 12-word seed phrase and you're always in control.

- Biometric Security — Lock your wallet with Face ID or Touch ID so only you can access your treasure.

- AI Agent Keys — A unique feature that lets you grant programmatic access to your wallet for AI agents and bots, with per-transaction and daily spending limits.

- Transaction History — A beautifully designed "Ship's Log" shows every payment with color-coded icons, editable memos, and fiat equivalents.

- Midnight & Daylight Modes — Switch between a sleek dark theme and a warm, sun-bleached light theme.

- Sound Effects — Hear the ship's bell when you receive sats and fire the cannons when you send them.

- Multi-Currency Display — View your balance in sats or fiat (USD, EUR, and more).

Powered by the Breez SDK on Bitcoin Mainnet, Buccaneer gives you a full Lightning node experience without the complexity. No channels to manage, no liquidity to worry about — just Bitcoin that works.

Download Buccaneer Wallet and claim your treasure today.
