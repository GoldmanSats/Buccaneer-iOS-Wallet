Buccaneer Wallet Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Our Commitment

Buccaneer Wallet is a self-custody Bitcoin Lightning wallet. Your privacy is fundamental to everything we build. We believe your financial data belongs to you and only you.

Data We Collect

None. Buccaneer Wallet does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind.

What Stays on Your Device

All sensitive information is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • Wallet seed phrase — stored in your device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain) and never transmitted
  • Transaction history — retrieved directly from the Lightning network, not logged by us
  • App preferences — theme, currency, and sound settings are stored locally on your device
  • AI Agent Keys — generated and stored on your device; you control access

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels. There are no third-party analytics libraries in the app. We have no interest in monitoring how you use your wallet.

No Accounts or Registration

Buccaneer Wallet does not require an account, email address, phone number, or any personal information to use. You create a wallet and start using it immediately.

Network Communications

The app communicates with the Bitcoin Lightning Network to process transactions. These communications are inherent to how Bitcoin works and are not logged or monitored by us. Exchange rate data is fetched from public APIs to display fiat equivalents of your balance.

Your Keys, Your Coins

Buccaneer Wallet is fully self-custodial. We never have access to your funds, your seed phrase, or your private keys. If you lose your seed phrase, we cannot recover your wallet. You are in complete control.

Children's Privacy

Buccaneer Wallet is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Given that we collect no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
